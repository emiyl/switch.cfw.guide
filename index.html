Hackable Firmwares

All firmwares (4.1.0 and under) are hackable with ReSwitched's TrustZoneHax exploits, jamais vu and deja vu. These exploits are unreleased and will be released once a CFW solution has been finished. SciresM has stated he will be focusing primarily on the Atmosphere-NX Switch CFW in the future until it's usable to the public.

If you are on 4.0.0 or 4.0.1, it's safe to update now to 4.1.0. If you are on 3.0.2 or below, do not update, as the lower firmware you are, the earlier you'll get CFW.

Blocking Updates
  1. Turn on your Switch and go to the Settings application
  2. Go to System
  3. Switch Auto-Update Software to OFF
  4. Go back and then go to Network settings
  5. Go to DNS settings
  6. Change DNS to Manual
  7. Change your Primary DNS to 173.255.238.217
  8. Change your Secondary DNS to 173.255.238.217

Deleting Downloaded Updates
  1. Hold down the Power Button
  2. Select Reboot
  3. If that doesn't work, reboot again but hold down Volume + and Volume - while rebooting
